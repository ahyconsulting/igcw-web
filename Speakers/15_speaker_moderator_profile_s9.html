Dr. Sukumar Devotta

Former Director

CSIR-National Environmental Engineering & Research Institute (NEERI), India

Bio

Dr Sukumar Devotta, Former Director NEERI (2003-2008), obtained his Ph. D. (1978) in Chemical Engineering from the University of Madras. After two years of teaching experience in A.C. College, he proceeded to do Post-Doctoral Fellowship in the University of Salford, UK during 1979-84. He also obtained his M.Sc. from the University of Salford (1984). Dr. Devotta began his journey of scientific research at NCL, Pune in 1984 and rose to the position of Director Grade Scientist and Head, Process Development Division (in 1999). Later he was the Director, NEERI, Nagpur during 2003-08. At NEERI, he was extensively involved in the variety of environmental issues and their mitigation. He has immensely contributed to the field of Energy, Environment and Green Technologies, particularly in industrial energy conservation, Alternatives to CFCs, Chemical process development, Process engineering simulation, and Global environmental policy and protocols. Dr. Devotta served and continues to serve in many prestigious National & International Committees and also act as consultant to leading Indian and international agencies, including UNEP Technical Option Committee (Since 1992), QCI EIA Consultant Accreditation Committee â€“ Chairman (Since 2016), GoI Steering Committee on Montreal Protocol (Since inception), Board of Directors of KIOCL (2006-9) and HOCL (2007-10); Supreme Court Monitoring Committee (SCMC) on Hazardous Waste; Chairman, Maharashtra State EAC (2011-13); DST National Programme on Carbon Sequestration (2011-13), DST Task Force on Green Chemistry (2008-13), Board Member- CPCB (2015-18), GIZ, INSA-IGBP Programme, UNDP, Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change (IPCC), World Bank Ozone Operations Research Group (OORG), and WHO (Short-term Expert). Dr. Devotta has done pioneering research in the field of energy and environment. At NCL, he was also involved in some prestigious projects involving process design, simulation and development. Dr. Devotta was awarded, as a team leader, 2003 & 2005 CSIR Technology of the Year Award. As an appreciation for the technical and scientific leadership in Environment Protection, Dr. Devotta was awarded the prestigious US Environment Protection Agency (USEPA) "Stratospheric Ozone Protection Award" for 1997; received commendation letters from the Executive Director of UNEP and President of Switzerland for his contributions to Montreal Protocol. As a member of IPCC, he also received the citations from IPCC, which was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize in 2007 as well as a commendation from then Prime Minister of India. Dr. Devotta has guided 25 students for Ph.D. and ME/M. Tech. in Chemical, Mechanical & Environmental Engineering. He has also co-authored many Books, Monograph and Chapters in international reference books. He has also published more then 250 research papers in international journals of repute. He was also the Regional Editor for Applied Thermal Engineering and Editorial Board Member of International Journal of Refrigeration, published by Elsevier, UK. Currently he is a member of the International Advisory Board of these two journals. He has 5 US patents as a co-inventor to his credit.
