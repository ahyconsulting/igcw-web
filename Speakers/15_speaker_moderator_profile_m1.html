Mr. Mahesh Chandak

Head of HSE – South Asia & HSE Business Partner for Crop Science Division-APAC

Bayer Cropscience, India

Bio

Mahesh Chandak has over 24+ years of diverse petrochemical process plant; Health, Safety, Environment (HSE), Human Rights and Sustainability experience. He has both industry and consulting experience in leading diverse teams across multiple continents with project experience in 20+ countries. He is recognized as HSE and Sustainability thought leader in various prestigious industry forums and appointed as jury member for several esteemed industry awards. In the past eight years at Monsanto (acquired by Bayer), the organization achieved a step change improvement HSE and Human Rights performance. He demonstrated ability to shape global HSE and sustainability strategy, partnering with regional teams to provide guidance and also lead execution at ground level. He has strong technical and execution excellence in Environmental Compliance, People and Process Safety, Occupational Medicine and Health, Wellness and Well-being, Resource Conservation, Vehicle Safety, HSE Management Systems, Human Rights, Community Outreach and Sustainability. In earlier tole at MWH (now part of Stantec), he executed over 500 Environmental, Safety and Sustainability projects for various multinational clients in 15 countries including India, Europe, Middle East, Asia and USA. Offered a wide spectrum of services including HSE Compliance Assurance of operating units; Mergers and Acquisitions Advisory Services – Phase I/II Environmental Site Assessments, Remediation Investigation/ Feasibility studies, Environmental legislation training, Environment Impact Assessments (EIA), Risk Assessment and HAZOP, Water Reuse and Waste Minimization studies. He led many environmental, social, working condition assessment studies for various suppliers and infrastructure projects.
