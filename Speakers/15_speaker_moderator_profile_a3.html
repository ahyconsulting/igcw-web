Mr. Alok Pandit

CEO

Equinox Software & Services Pvt. Ltd., India

Bio

Alok brings over 30 years’ experience in plant operations (Petroleum Refinery), MES, Process Simulation, LP Modeling, and industrial process optimization. A Chemical Process Engineer Alok learned the craft at Hinditron and Onward Technologies (Indian Representative of ChemShare and AspenTech respectively). Later, Alok led and managed the Process Software division at Onward with the agency of Setpoint and AspenTech. Alok coordinated all business management tasks for setting up the OTS team at Onward. Those experiences underscored intense challenges bringing his entrepreneurial mind-set together to improve the overall profitability of the Process Group at Onward. Consequently, in 2000 Alok founded Hyprotech India at Pune as a 100% subsidiary of Hyprotech Canada and managed it efficiently until it was acquired by AspenTech in 2002. Post-acquisition Alok was retained as the head of business for South Asia by AspenTech, further growing to the role of Vice President, responsible for South East Asia, Australia & New Zealand apart from SAARC Region. Finally, in 2008 Alok quit his position at Equinox to setup Equinox, along with few other friends, a company that he is leading since its inception. At Equinox, Alok is leading Equinox’s foray into emerging technologies, like IIOT, Real Time Performance Monitoring and Optimization and Performance Analytics and the development of new software products/solutions.
