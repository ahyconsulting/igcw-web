Mr. Manjit Singh

Associate Director - Corporate Sustainability

Centrient Pharmaceuticals, India

Bio

Manjit Singh has about 32 years’ experience in pharmaceutical manufacturing operations. Currently, Manjit is member of Sustainability Core Group which steers corporate sustainability. He leads global operational sustainability. His responsibilities include sustainability vision, strategy, setting up sustainability goals, implementation of sustainability goals at own operations, suppliers and CMO’s. He represents Centrient at external sustainability platforms i.e. PSCI, AMR IA, Eco Vadis, CDP and other organizations. He is Centrient lead for PSCI and PSCI lead for India. He is bachelor’s in chemistry from Punjabi University. Post Graduate Diploma in Operations, Indira Gandhi National Open University, Delhi.
