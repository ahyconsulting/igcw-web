Dr. Rajiv Banavali

Global Vice President - Research & Technology

Huntsman Corporation, Singapore

Bio

Rajiv Banavali is the VP, R&T for Textile effects division of Huntsman Corporation. Reporting to Rohit Aggarwal, President, the role leads research, development and product commercialization initiatives for the division. The job has responsibility for the leadership, actions and development of a technology team based in multiple locations globally, along with budget, building global organization, leading the portfolio management process for all technology, managing the new product development process using Stage Gate discipline, and supporting technology initiatives across key strategic growth platforms. Rajiv joined Huntsman from Honeywell, where for 10 years he held various roles of increasing responsibility in R&D to the Chief of Technology Officer for the Advanced Materials business in PMT. He has more than 80 publications, including 40 patents/applications, and has developed and commercialized several new processes, products and applications, with high profitability and sales in excess of $5 billion. His areas of expertise are in Dyes, catalysis, polymer products, organic synthesis, process development and a variety of applications in Films, Fibers, Additives, Fine chemicals, Biofuels, Photovoltaic, Li battery, and water treatment.
