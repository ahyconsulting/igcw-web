Abstract
Air monitoring solution for ion analysis by using Reagent free Ion Chromatography

Accurate, precise , sensitive and very reproducible determination of Anions and Organic acids, Cations and Amines by using Reagent Free Ion Chromatography. RFIC systems have redefined ion chromatography by making it simpler, more powerful, and more reliable. Just add water, and the RFIC system delivers superior results while saving your time and labor. The entire process only requires deionized water for online electrolytic eluent generation ( EG), eliminating the need to handle any acids or bases and ropes to green chemistry as well. In addition to saving time, labor, and operating costs, RFIC-EG systems eliminate errors associated with manual eluent preparation. This effectively improves method reproducibility, day to day, week to week, lab to lab which allow environmental scientist to work unattended with reliable technique.
