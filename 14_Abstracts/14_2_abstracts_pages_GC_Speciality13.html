Abstract
Close Circuit Spray Dryers for Pharmaceutical & Chemical Industry, highlighting energy efficiency & modern Safety features.

Pharmaceutical industry is currently undergoing a rapid transformation and innovative pharmaceutical technologies, such as spray dryers, are helping to set the pace For example, new sophisticated drug delivery technologies have been developed to address the changing demands of the industry. These new technologies require greater performance from product particles than they once did. Particles, which were once simply carriers for the drug, are now integral parts of complex delivery systems and need to exhibit advanced functional properties. Spray drying has the ability to create particles with the advanced characteristics required by new pharmaceutical technologies. Spray drying allows particle engineering to develop the particle’s functional characteristics. During spray drying, the feed is atomized forming liquid droplets in the drying chamber. A droplet can only dry from its surface; therefore as the droplet dries, the particle surface becomes enriched with the more soluble ingredient and the less soluble ingredient is left inside the particle. This is how microencapsulation occurs. Presentation cover’s: • Spray Dryer working principle/ stages of Spray Drying • Drying product soluble in solvent other than water • Close circuit dryers for Pharma & Chemical Industry – Application & Advantages • Energy efficiency • Safety systems • Pharma Documentation Level • Range of small scale plants
