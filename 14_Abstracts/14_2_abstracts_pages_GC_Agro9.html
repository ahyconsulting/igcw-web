Abstract
Smashing the Shackles of Mass Transfer

Most batch reactors, anywhere in the world, operate under a ‘Mass Transfer Controlled’ regime. Worded differently, it says that, All reactors in the world can see significant output enhancement, if the restraints of Mass Transfer are unshackled. This presentation talks about the deleterious effects of poor mass transfer. It discusses how becoming free of a mass controlled regime is the surest way to Green Engineering. The benefits of this will automatically become obvious More importantly, the talk provides several easy ways to boost the Mass Transfer in heritage (existing) reactors and discusses the ways to design new Reaction systems to Smash the Shackles of Mass Transfer. It also briefly touches on how to evaluate the reaction progress on the path to becoming free of the constraints
