Abstract
Contributions from NCL in Green Catalysis

Development of greener and atom efficient solutions to industrial processes is a need of an hour due to strict environmental laws and for sustainable future. In this view, CSIR-National Chemical Laboratory has undertaken several initiatives in the area of catalysis, reaction engineering, polymer synthesis etc. CSIR-NCL also has historically participated in the National missions and is at the forefront in the present day too. To name few developments; CSIR-NCL is active in the synthesis of DME, water splitting, import substituents, biomass conversion, CO2 utilization, bio derived polymers etc. During the talk, case studies regarding these topics will be presented along with overall initiatives CSIR-NCL has taken in this area.
