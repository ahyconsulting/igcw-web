Abstract
Pfizer’s Green Journey – Working for a Sustainable Future

Green Chemistry is an integral part of drug development process at Pfizer and has played a fundamental role in developing sustainable alternatives for traditional chemical synthesis operations. For last two decades the Pfizer Green Chemistry program has led the promotion and development of technologies and scientific advances in the design of chemical processes that reduce and/or eliminate the use of hazardous substances and waste while maintaining the chemical process economics. This presentation will provide an overview of the Pfizer Green Chemistry program and the impact it has for the last two decades in delivering greener and cost-effective processes for the manufacturing of drugs.
