Abstract
Introduction to the ACS Green Chemistry Institute Pharmaceutical Round Table (ACS GCIPR): MSD Perspective

As a founding member of the ACS Green Chemistry Institute Pharmaceutical Round Table in 2005, MSD has been actively involved in all aspects of the program over the last 14 years. The ACS GCIPR has 4 main objectives: 1. Inform and influence research agenda 2. Define and deliver tools for innovation 3. Promote education and training 4. Enable global collaboration This presentation will provide an overview of the ACS GCIPR, tools developed and ongoing initiatives. An MSD perspective on the value of the partnership to drive green and sustainable chemistry development across pharma and other sectors will be provided.
