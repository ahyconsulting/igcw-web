Our goal at MSD mission is to translate breakthroughs in biomedical research into meaningful new therapeutics and vaccines that improve and extend the lives of people, worldwide. The pharmaceutical industry is expected to meet the needs of patients around the world at a cost they can afford while minimizing our environmental footprint.
