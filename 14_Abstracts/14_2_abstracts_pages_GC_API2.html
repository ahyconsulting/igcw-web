Abstract
Business Case for Green and Sustainable Chemistry Development at MSD: A Personal Perspective

The mission at MSD is to translate breakthroughs in fundamental biomedical research into meaningful new therapeutics and vaccines that improve and extend the lives of people, worldwide, while reducing costs and our environmental footprint. The strive for scientific excellence has always been an integral part of the drug development philosophy at MSD. Our goal is to use innovations in chemistry and engineering to deliver the best science and the most efficient, green and sustainable commercial manufacturing processes. This presentation will highlight, using several case studies, why Green Chemistry and Engineering is important for MSD, tools & technologies to drive innovative science, the beneficial economic and environmental impacts of green chemistry and the positive impact of wide-ranging collaborations. I will provide a personal perspective of the MSD Green Chemistry development strategy and the overwhelmingly positive impact it has made in delivering the best science, the most green, sustainable and economically viable processes for the commercial manufacture of life improving drugs.
